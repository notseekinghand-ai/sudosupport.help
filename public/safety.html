Safety & Sovereignty

Our operational commitment to your digital and intellectual security.

Our Guiding Principle: You Own Your Iron

The entire philosophy of SudoSupport is built on the principle of sovereignty. This extends beyond technology choices and into our operational security and how we handle your information. Your data, your infrastructure, and your intellectual property are yours alone.

Data Sovereignty by Default

  • No Cloud-Based Data Storage: We do not store your sensitive information on third-party cloud services. All project-related data resides on encrypted, self-hosted infrastructure under our direct control.
  • On-Premise First: Our solutions are designed to run on your hardware. We advocate for and implement on-premise AI, private cloud, and self-hosted monitoring to ensure your data never needs to leave your walls.
  • Zero Telemetry: The open-source software we deploy, such as Fedora and RHEL, does not contain the invasive telemetry common in proprietary systems. We do not monitor your day-to-day activities.

Operational Security (OPSEC)

  • End-to-End Encryption: All communications are conducted over encrypted channels.
  • Principle of Least Privilege: Access to systems is granted on a strictly need-to-know basis.
  • Use of Open Source: Our reliance on auditable, open-source code means that the tools we use are transparent and can be vetted by the global security community.

Financial Sovereignty

Our use of L2 payment rails like Base is a deliberate choice. It minimizes our reliance on the traditional banking system, which is susceptible to censorship and control. This ensures that our economic relationship is as resilient and sovereign as our technical solutions.

Your Responsibility

True sovereignty is a partnership. We provide the tools and expertise, but ultimate security rests on strong internal practices. We work with our clients to establish robust password policies, user access controls, and security awareness training.
